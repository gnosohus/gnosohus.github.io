Bio

Suho is a visual communication artist working across diverse practices including branding, UI/UX, digital design, and advertising. His practice focuses on creating meaningful visual experiences that connect design and people through storytelling.

He works with various clients including tech startups, design studios, and museums and art events, exploring how design can shape perception and bring clarity to complex ideas — often blending commercial and cultural expressions through a minimalist yet human-centered approach.
